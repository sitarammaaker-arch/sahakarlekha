# SahakarLekha Research Task 2.5 — Operational Workflows of Indian Cooperative Societies

**Scope:** Real business workflows only. No software, no competitors, no features, no improvements, no accounting theory. Documents the existing real-world process. **[NV]** = Needs Verification. **Types:** All = all eight · Credit = PACS/credit-thrift/MSCS-credit/coop-banks · Trading = PACS/Marketing/Consumer/Dairy/Industrial. Accounting entries are high-level only.

---

### 1. Member Registration
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Objective | Admit a new member per bye-laws |
| Trigger | Membership application received |
| Steps | 1 Applicant submits form + fee + KYC 2 Verify eligibility & area of operation 3 Place before committee 4 Committee approves/rejects 5 Allot membership no. & shares 6 Collect share/entrance fee 7 Issue receipt & share certificate 8 Record in Member & Share registers 9 Record nominee 10 Communicate to member |
| Responsible role | Secretary + Managing Committee |
| Input documents | Application form, ID/address proof, fee |
| Output documents | Approval, share certificate, receipt |
| Accounting entries | Dr Cash/Bank; Cr Share Capital; Cr Entrance/Admission fee |
| Registers updated | Member Register (Form I), Share Register (Form J), Nominee register, Cash book |
| Approvals | Managing committee |
| Compliance checkpoints | Eligibility; area of operation; nominee recorded |
| Common mistakes | Ineligible admission; nominee missing; register not updated |
| Applicable types | All |
| Official reference | MCS Rule 32 / Form I; bye-laws |
| NV | Form/section varies by state **[NV]** |
