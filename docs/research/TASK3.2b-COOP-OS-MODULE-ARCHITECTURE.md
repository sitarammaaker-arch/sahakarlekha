## Consolidated architecture notes (from prior research)
- **Core spine (every society):** Member Management, Share Capital, Accounting, Cash & Bank, Audit, Compliance, Reports — these plus Loans/Deposits (Credit) are non-negotiable; reflects the ~70–80% shared-functionality finding.
- **Activity modules (per type):** Procurement/Inventory/Sales (Trading), Loans/Deposits (Credit), Payroll (staff/Labour), Investments (surplus-holding), Budget (large/federations) — enabled per society's business.
- **Highest-impact correctness dependencies:** Accounting ↔ Reports (formula consistency) and Cash & Bank / Loans (data integrity, NPA) — the two failure classes flagged in prior research; Audit + Document Management + Compliance form the statutory-evidence backbone.
- **AI is a downstream layer:** every AI opportunity depends on clean master + transaction data and the audit trail — a readiness posture, not a current module.
