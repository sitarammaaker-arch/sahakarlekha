## Topic matrix (8 topics)

| Topic | Purpose / Law | Applicable types | Registration + threshold | Returns + frequency | Due dates | Records / registers | Common mistakes | Penalties | Authority |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **GST** | Tax on supply of goods/services · CGST/SGST/IGST Act 2017 | Trading & taxable-service; Housing above threshold | GSTIN mandatory above **₹40L goods / ₹20L services** (state-variant) | GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9 (annual) | GSTR-1 11th (or QRMP 13th); GSTR-3B 20th; GSTR-9 31 Dec | Tax invoices, GST register, ITC ledger | Wrong ITC; output-input mismatch; RCM missed; late filing | Late fee ₹50/day; interest 18% p.a.; ITC reversal | CBIC / GSTN |
| **TDS** | Tax deducted at source · Income-tax Act 1961 | All (deductors) | **TAN** mandatory | 24Q (salary), 26Q (non-salary), 27Q | Deposit by **7th**; returns 31 Jul/31 Oct/31 Jan/31 May; Form 16/16A after | TDS register, challans, certificates | Not deducted/late deposit; PAN error; return mismatch | Interest 1%/1.5% p.m.; late fee ₹200/day (234E); expense disallowance | CBDT |
| **Income Tax** | Direct tax on income · IT Act 1961 (s.80P; 115BAD/BAE) | All | **PAN**; tax audit if turnover crosses s.44AB limit | ITR-5; tax audit report 3CA/3CB-3CD if applicable | ITR **31 Oct (audit) / 31 Jul (non-audit)**; audit report 30 Sept; advance tax quarterly | Books, audited accounts, computation | 80P claimed with 115BAD; audit missed; advance tax unpaid | Interest 234A/B/C; penalty; prosecution | CBDT |
| **Payroll compliance** | Statutory payroll deduction/remittance · Wages/PF/ESI/PT/IT Acts | All with staff; Labour | Per component (TAN/PF/ESI/PT) | 24Q, PF ECR, ESI, PT return | Wages by 7th; PF/ESI by **15th** | Salary register, muster, pay slips, challans | Wages ≠ muster; dues not remitted | Interest/damages per Act | EPFO/ESIC/State/CBDT |
| **Professional Tax** | State tax on profession/employment · State PT Acts | All with employees (**state-applicable only**) | PTEC + PTRC registration | PT return (monthly/annual, state-variant) | State-specific | PT register, challans | Not deducted; wrong slab | Interest + penalty | State commercial-tax dept |
| **EPF** | Provident fund · EPF & MP Act 1952 | Establishments with **20+ employees** | Mandatory at 20+; wage ceiling ₹15,000 | Monthly **ECR** | ECR/contribution by **15th** | PF register, ECR, UAN records | Late deposit; wrong wage base | Interest 12% + damages 5–25% (s.14B) | EPFO |
| **ESI** | Health/social-security insurance · ESI Act 1948 | Establishments **10+ employees** (state-notified), wages ≤ **₹21,000/mo** | Mandatory at 10+ (some states 20) | Monthly contribution; **half-yearly return** | Contribution by **15th**; half-yearly Nov & May | ESI register, contribution, accident register | Non-coverage; late deposit | Interest 12% + damages | ESIC |
| **Labour-law registers** | Statutory labour records · Contract Labour (R&A), Wages, Bonus, new Labour Codes | With employees/contract labour (esp. **Labour, Industrial**) | Licence if contract labour engaged | Annual/half-yearly labour returns | State/Act-specific | Muster roll, wage, deductions, OT, fines, advances, bonus registers | Registers not maintained | Fine / prosecution | Labour Commissioner |

## Income-tax rate note (cooperatives)
| Regime | Rate |
|---|---|
| Normal slab | Up to ₹10,000: 10% · ₹10,001–20,000: 20% · above ₹20,000: 30% (+ surcharge + 4% cess) |
| Section 115BAD (opt-in) | Flat **22%** (+10% surcharge +4% cess); **no s.80P / most deductions** |
| Section 115BAE (new mfg co-ops) | Flat 15% (conditions apply) **[NV]** |
| AMT | 15% of adjusted total income (115BAD/BAE opt-outs generally exempt) |
| s.80P deduction | Available to eligible societies (credit-to-members, etc.) unless 115BAD/BAE opted |
