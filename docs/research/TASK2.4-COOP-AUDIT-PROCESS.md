# SahakarLekha Research Task 2.4 — Audit Process in Indian Cooperative Societies

**Scope:** Audit knowledge & accounting controls only. No software, no competitors, no features, no legal history, no implementation advice. **[NV]** = Needs Verification. **Types:** All = all eight cooperative types · Credit = PACS/credit-thrift/MSCS-credit/coop-banks · Trading = PACS/Marketing/Consumer/Dairy/Industrial.

## 1. Five audit types (field × type matrix)

| Field | Statutory Audit | Internal Audit | Government Inspection | Special Audit | Concurrent Audit |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Independent annual verification; true & fair view; audit classification A/B/C/D | Ongoing internal check on transactions & controls | Registrar examines constitution, working, financial condition | Targeted audit of suspected irregularity/complaint | Real-time transaction-level check to catch errors immediately |
| **Applicable types** | All | Banks, large societies/federations, big PACS, MSCS | All (on trigger) | All (on order/complaint) | Cooperative banks (UCB/DCCB/StCB); large credit societies **[NV]** |
| **Conducted by** | Registrar-empanelled/departmental auditor or CA (CA for MSCS) | Internal auditor/staff/firm (committee-appointed) | Registrar or authorised officer | Auditor appointed by Registrar | External CA firm / internal team per RBI norms |
| **Frequency** | Annually, ≤6 months of FY close | Continuous (monthly–quarterly) | On order/complaint (not routine) | Ad hoc (on order) | Continuous (daily/ongoing) |
| **Major documents** | Bye-laws, prior audit report, minutes, bank statements, vouchers, balance confirmations | Vouchers, registers, policies, prior internal reports | Books, minutes, cash, securities, correspondence | Complaint/order + records under scrutiny | Daily vouchers, transaction logs, loan/KYC files |
| **Records checked** | Cash book, general/personal ledgers, DCB, loan/stock/investment/member/share registers, vouchers | Same subset, ongoing test-check | All books + cash + securities + stock | Records tied to the allegation (loans/cash/procurement) | Daily cash, loans, deposits, KYC |
| **Statements checked** | R&P, Trading/P&L, Balance Sheet, DCB | Trial balance, interim statements | Balance Sheet, P&L, net worth | Relevant statement segment | Daily/periodic MIS |
| **Common procedures** | Vouching, ledger scrutiny, balance confirmation, NPA classification & provisioning, physical verification, valuation | Test-checking, control testing, reconciliation | Cash verification, record inspection, net-worth assessment | Forensic tracing of specific transactions | Real-time voucher/limit/KYC verification |
| **Common findings** | High overdues/NPA, missing vouchers, non-reconciliation, stock shortages | Control gaps, unreconciled sub-ledgers, unauthorised entries | Governance lapses, cash shortage, capital erosion | Fraud/misappropriation, ineligible loans, fund diversion | Limit breaches, KYC gaps, misposting |
| **Objection reasons** | Poor documentation, statutory non-compliance, weak internal controls | Weak controls, override of procedures | Non-compliance, mismanagement | Confirmed irregularity | Real-time deviation from norms |
| **High-risk areas** | Loans/NPA, cash, stock/procurement, investments, related-party | Cash, inventory, approvals | Deposits, capital adequacy, related-party | Loans, procurement, cash, siphoning | Loans, deposits, KYC/AML |
| **Applicable laws** | State Act (e.g. MCS s.81); MSCS Act (CA audit); audit manual | Bye-laws; RBI/NABARD (banks) | State Act (e.g. MCS s.83/84 inquiry/inspection); MSCS Ch. VIII | State Act special-audit/inquiry provision; MSCS Ch. VIII | RBI concurrent-audit norms (UCBs) |
| **Official reference** | MCS Act 1960; MSCS Act 2002; state audit manual | Bye-laws; RBI/NABARD circulars | MCS Act s.83/84; MSCS Act Ch. VIII (15-day notice) | MCS Act (inquiry) s.83; MSCS Act | RBI Master Directions / concurrent-audit circulars |
| **NV** | Grading criteria & report-part format vary by state **[NV]** | Compulsory threshold varies **[NV]** | Section numbers/triggers vary **[NV]** | Special-audit section varies by state **[NV]** | Threshold & non-bank applicability **[NV]** |

## A. Top recurring audit objections (highest → lowest frequency)

| Rank | Objection | Why it happens | Impact | Applicable types |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | High overdue loans / poor recovery | Weak follow-up, willful default, no DCB discipline | Liquidity strain; NPA rise | Credit |
| 2 | Inadequate NPA classification & provisioning | Norms not applied; income overstated | Inflated profit; capital erosion | Credit |
| 3 | Overdue interest booked as income | Accrual on non-performing loans | Overstated surplus/dividend | Credit |
| 4 | Missing/insufficient vouchers & documentation | Poor record discipline | Weak audit trail; suspense | All |
| 5 | Bank/DCCB non-reconciliation | Reconciliation not done regularly | Misstated balances; fraud risk | All |
| 6 | Stock shortages / dead-stock not written off | No physical verification; pilferage | Overstated assets | Trading |
| 7 | Cash discrepancies / balance not verified | No daily cash verification | Misappropriation risk | All |
| 8 | Statutory dues short/late (TDS/GST/PF/ESI) | Compliance lapse | Interest/penalty | All (as applicable) |
| 9 | Investments outside permitted list / interest not accrued | Norm ignorance | Regulatory breach; understated income | All holding investments |
| 10 | Member/share register not updated; capital mismatch | Records neglected | Governance & audit-trail gaps | All |
| 11 | AGM/audit-rectification/returns delayed | Governance neglect | Non-compliance; penalties | All |
| 12 | Ineligible/benami loans; related-party diversion | Governance failure | Fraud; loss | Credit, MSCS |

## B. Common accounting mistakes (by area)

| Area | Common mistake |
|---|---|
| Cash | Negative/unverified cash balance; delayed write-up; petty cash unvouched |
| Bank | No reconciliation; stale cheques not reversed; interest not accounted |
| Inventory | No physical verification; wrong valuation (not cost/NRV); expired/dead stock not written off |
| Procurement | Agency/pool accounts unreconciled; qty/quality not verified |
| GST | Wrong ITC claim; output-input mismatch; RCM missed; return delays |
| TDS | Not deducted / late deposit (post-7th); PAN errors; return mismatch |
| Assets | Ghost assets; no physical verification; wrong depreciation rate/method |
| Payroll | Wages ≠ muster roll; PF/ESI/TDS not remitted; overtime unsupported |
| Member accounts | Register not updated; nominee missing; sub-ledger ≠ control |
| Share capital | Share ledger ≠ balance sheet; certificates unissued; transfer premium above ceiling |
| Loans | NPA misclassified; overdue interest as income; missing loan bonds; ineligible loans |
| Journal entries | Unauthorised JVs; no narration/support; adjustment misuse |
| Closing entries | Interest/depreciation/provisions not accrued; surplus appropriation (reserve/education/dividend) wrong or omitted |

## C. Year-end closing checklist

| Activity | Responsible person | Timing | Documents required |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cut-off & write up all books; close cash/bank | Secretary/Accountant | FY-end (31 Mar) | Cash book, ledgers, bank statements |
| Bank & DCCB reconciliation | Accountant | Immediately post FY-end | Bank statements, pass sheets |
| Physical stock verification & valuation | Store-keeper + Committee | At/near FY-end | Stock register, valuation sheet |
| Fixed-asset verification & depreciation | Accountant | Year-end | Asset/depreciation register |
| Interest accrual (loans & deposits); NPA classification & provisioning | Accountant | Year-end | Loan ledger, DCB, deposit registers |
| Balance confirmations (borrowers/creditors/debtors) | Secretary | Year-end | Personal ledgers, confirmations |
| Prepare Receipts & Payments, Trading/P&L, Balance Sheet | Accountant | Within statutory days (e.g. 45) of FY close | Trial balance, ledgers |
| Surplus appropriation (reserve ≥25%, education fund, dividend within cap) | Committee | Draft before AGM | P&L, reserve computation |
| Arrange statutory audit | Committee | ≤6 months of FY close | Full books, prior audit report |
| Hold AGM; adopt accounts & audit; declare dividend | Committee (Chairman/Secretary) | By ~30 Sept | Audited accounts, audit report, agenda |
| File annual return & audit-rectification report to Registrar | Secretary | Post-AGM (state window) | Return form, audit report, minutes |
