## Expansion domains (D27–D30) — reach 300+ via combinatorial expansion

The clusters above (C001–C230) are the **canonical spine**. The registry crosses **300+** by
expanding two systematic axes (each combination = a real, distinct page):

### D27 · Society-type × Topic matrix `⚠️`
For each of 12 society types × {accounting basics, COA, loans/stock as relevant, audit, returns,
GST, year-end}, generate a tailored cluster. **12 × ~6 = 72 clusters (C231–C302).**
Example nodes: "Dairy society year-end checklist", "PACS GST applicability", "Housing society audit
objections", "Consumer society stock valuation", "Credit society NPA norms". (Build only where a
real distinction exists; otherwise canonicalize to the generic cluster — see [07 §Canonical](07-seo-engine.md).)

### D28 · State × Society-type landing matrix
`/cooperative-software/:state` × top society types → geo-commercial pages (programmatic). Governed by
[07-seo-engine.md](07-seo-engine.md); thin-content guardrails apply (must add a state-specific fact, not
just swap a name). **~28 states × 3 priority types, phased — start with 8 states (C303–C326).**

### D29 · Calculator & Template landing clusters
Each entry in [10-calculators.md](10-calculators.md) and [09-template-library.md](09-template-library.md)
gets a landing cluster (tool + explainer + download). **~40 clusters (C327–C366).**

### D30 · Comparison & "vs" clusters
"SahakarLekha vs Tally / Excel / manual", "SLM vs WDV", "cooperative vs company", "cash vs accrual",
"FIFO vs weighted avg", society-type comparisons. **~20 clusters (C367–C386).**

> **Registry total: 230 canonical + ~156 expansion = ~386 clusters (>300 ✅).**
> Expansion clusters are *generated*, not hand-written, from the spine + matrices — preventing
> orphan/thin pages while guaranteeing coverage. Governance: never publish a matrix cell that
> merely renames a generic page; it must carry a genuine type/state-specific fact (`⚠️ NEV`).
