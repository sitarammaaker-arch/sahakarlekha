# KI-000025 · Accounting (लेखांकन)

**Definition:** Accounting is the systematic process of recording, classifying, summarising, and reporting a society's financial transactions so that its financial position and performance can be understood and verified.

**Plain-language explanation:** Accounting is keeping an organised record of every rupee in and out, so anyone can see how the society is doing and trust the numbers.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** लेखांकन यानी समिति के हर लेन-देन को क्रम से लिखना, छाँटना, जोड़ना और रिपोर्ट बनाना — ताकि पता चले कि समिति की आर्थिक हालत कैसी है और हिसाब पर भरोसा किया जा सके। सीधे शब्दों में — पैसे का साफ़-सुथरा हिसाब रखना।

**English explanation:** The discipline of recording and reporting financial transactions in a structured way, producing reliable statements about a society's money.

**Why it matters:** It is the foundation of everything SahakarLekha does. Good accounting means clean audits, correct reports, and trustworthy decisions.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "Accounting is only for big organisations." → Even the smallest society needs it.
- ❌ "Accounting = just maths." → It is mainly organised recording and reporting, not heavy calculation.
