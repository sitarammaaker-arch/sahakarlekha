# KI-000098 · Expense Dictionary (व्यय शब्दकोश)

**Definition:** An expense dictionary is a reference list of common expense heads with short notes on what each covers — helping users record each cost under the right head (e.g. is it "Repairs", "Stationery", or "Professional Fees"?).

**Plain-language explanation:** It is a list of expense names with "what goes here", so you always put a cost under the correct head.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** व्यय शब्दकोश आम खर्च-शीर्षों की एक सूची है, जिसमें हर शीर्ष के बारे में छोटी टिप्पणी होती है कि उसमें क्या आता है — ताकि उपयोगकर्ता हर खर्च को सही शीर्ष के नीचे दर्ज करे (जैसे यह "मरम्मत" है, "स्टेशनरी" है, या "व्यावसायिक शुल्क")।

**English explanation:** A guide to expense heads and what each includes, reducing mis-classification and keeping the Profit & Loss / I&E clean.

**Why it matters:** Mis-posting an expense to the wrong head distorts reports and budgets. A dictionary keeps classification consistent and correct.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "Any expense head will do." → The right head keeps the P&L/I&E and budget meaningful.
- ❌ "An expense dictionary is only for accountants." → New staff use it to avoid common mis-postings.
