# KI-000314 · Dashboard (डैशबोर्ड)

**Definition:** A dashboard is the at-a-glance home screen of the software that summarises the society's key numbers — cash and bank balances, members, income/expense, dues — so leaders can see the society's status quickly without opening each report.

**Plain-language explanation:** It is the home screen showing the most important numbers at a glance — cash, bank, members, surplus — so you know the society's status instantly.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** डैशबोर्ड सॉफ्टवेयर का मुख्य (होम) स्क्रीन है जो समिति के ज़रूरी आँकड़े एक नज़र में दिखाता है — रोकड़ व बैंक शेष, सदस्य, आय/व्यय, बकाया। इससे नेतृत्व बिना हर रिपोर्ट खोले समिति की स्थिति तुरंत देख लेता है।

**English explanation:** A summary home screen of key metrics, giving managers and the board a quick health view of the society.

**Why it matters:** It turns scattered numbers into a quick status view — helpful for chairmen and managers who need the headline, not every ledger.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "The dashboard replaces detailed reports." → It summarises; detailed reports are still there when needed.
- ❌ "Only accountants use it." → It is most useful for chairmen/managers wanting a quick overview.
