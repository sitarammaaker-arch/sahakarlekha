# KI-000086 · Day Book (डे बुक)

**Definition:** A day book is a date-wise record of all transactions entered, listing every voucher in chronological order regardless of account — a daily diary of the society's entries.

**Plain-language explanation:** The day book is a date-order list of everything entered that day or period — handy to review all activity in sequence.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** डे बुक (day book) सभी लेन-देन का तारीखवार रिकॉर्ड है — इसमें हर वाउचर समय के क्रम में दिखता है, चाहे खाता कोई भी हो। आसान शब्दों में — समिति की एंट्रियों की "दैनिक डायरी"। किसी दिन/अवधि में क्या-क्या हुआ, एक नज़र में देख सकते हैं।

**English explanation:** A chronological listing of all vouchers; complements the ledger (which is account-wise) by giving a time-ordered view.

**Why it matters:** The day book is the quickest way to review a day's or period's activity and to spot a missing or wrong entry.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "Day book and ledger show the same thing." → Day book is by date; ledger is by account.
- ❌ "Day book is only for cash." → It can list all voucher types, not only cash.
