# KI-000009 · Society Types — Overview (सहकारी समिति के प्रकार)

**Definition:** Cooperative societies are classified by the main activity they perform for members — such as credit, agriculture (PACS), dairy, consumer, marketing, housing, labour, processing, and apex/federation bodies.

**Plain-language explanation:** "Type" simply tells you what the society mainly does — lend money, sell milk, run a shop, build houses, and so on. The activity decides which records and reports matter most.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** समिति का "प्रकार" यह बताता है कि वह मुख्य रूप से क्या काम करती है — जैसे कर्ज़ देना (साख समिति), खेती से जुड़ी (PACS), दूध (डेयरी), दुकान (उपभोक्ता), बिक्री-खरीद (विपणन), मकान (हाउसिंग) आदि। काम के हिसाब से ही समिति के रजिस्टर और रिपोर्ट तय होते हैं।

**English explanation:** A grouping of cooperatives by primary function (credit, agricultural, dairy, consumer, marketing, housing, labour, processing, federation), which in turn shapes their accounting and compliance focus.

**Why it matters:** The society type decides which SahakarLekha features a society uses most (e.g. loans for credit societies, stock for consumer societies) — and guides setup.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "All societies keep identical books." → The core is the same, but the emphasis (loans vs stock vs procurement) differs by type.
- ❌ "A society can only do one activity." → Multi-purpose societies (MPACS) combine several.
