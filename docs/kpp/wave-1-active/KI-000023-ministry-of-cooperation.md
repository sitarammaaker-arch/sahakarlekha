# KI-000023 · Ministry of Cooperation (सहकारिता मंत्रालय)

**Definition:** The Ministry of Cooperation is the Government of India ministry dedicated to strengthening the cooperative movement — driving national cooperative policy, schemes, and modernisation efforts such as PACS computerisation.

**Plain-language explanation:** It is the central government ministry that promotes and modernises cooperatives across India.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** सहकारिता मंत्रालय भारत सरकार का वह मंत्रालय है जो सहकारी आंदोलन को मज़बूत करने के लिए बना है — राष्ट्रीय सहकारी नीति, योजनाएँ और आधुनिकीकरण (जैसे PACS कम्प्यूटरीकरण) इसी के तहत आते हैं। ("सहकार से समृद्धि" इसका ध्येय-वाक्य है।)

**English explanation:** The dedicated central ministry for the cooperative sector, leading policy, schemes, and digital modernisation.

**Why it matters:** Knowing the central body behind cooperative policy frames why digitisation and PACS computerisation are pushed — directly relevant to going digital with SahakarLekha.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "The Ministry registers each society." → Registration is by the state Registrar (RCS); the Ministry sets national direction.
- ❌ "It runs the societies." → It promotes/supports; societies stay member-run.
