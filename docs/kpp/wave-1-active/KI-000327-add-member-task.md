# KI-000327 · Add a Member (सदस्य कैसे जोड़ें)

**Definition:** Adding a member is the task of recording a new member in the society's register in the software — entering their name and basic details, share information, and any opening dues — so the society's membership records stay complete and current.

**Plain-language explanation:** It is the simple task of entering a new member into the software — name, details, and shares — so they appear in the member register.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** सदस्य जोड़ना यानी सॉफ्टवेयर में समिति के रजिस्टर में नया सदस्य दर्ज करना — उसका नाम व बुनियादी जानकारी, अंश (शेयर) की जानकारी, और कोई प्रारंभिक बकाया डालना। इससे समिति का सदस्य-रिकॉर्ड पूरा व अद्यतन रहता है।

**English explanation:** The step-by-step task of registering a new member, the first thing most societies do after setup.

**Why it matters:** Members are the centre of a society; a complete, correct member register underpins shares, dividends, and voting.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "Anyone using the society is automatically in the register." → A member must be formally added.
- ❌ "Member details can be left incomplete." → Complete details avoid problems later (nomination, dues, contact).
