# KI-000337 · Close the Financial Year (साल कैसे बंद करें)

**Definition:** Closing the financial year is the end-of-year task of finalising the books — ensuring all entries are made and reconciled, generating the final accounts, and locking the year so figures stay stable for audit and carry forward correctly as next year's opening.

**Plain-language explanation:** It is the year-end task: make sure everything is entered and reconciled, produce the final reports, and lock the year so it can't change — then next year opens from the right figures.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** साल बंद करना साल के अंत का काम है — यह पक्का करना कि सभी एंट्री हो चुकीं व मिलान हो गया, अंतिम खाते तैयार करना, और साल को लॉक करना ताकि आँकड़े ऑडिट के लिए स्थिर रहें और अगले साल के opening में सही जुड़ें। (इस प्रक्रिया का विस्तृत/सांविधिक पक्ष अलग, validation-योग्य विषय है।)

**English explanation:** The step-by-step year-end task (finalise → final accounts → lock); a clean close makes audit smooth and next year's opening correct.

**Why it matters:** A proper year-end keeps figures stable for audit and ensures the opening balances of the next year are right — avoiding errors that would otherwise carry forward.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "Just start the new year; the old one sorts itself." → The year should be finalised and locked deliberately.
- ❌ "Locking can be undone casually." → A lock protects audited figures; changing it is a controlled action.
