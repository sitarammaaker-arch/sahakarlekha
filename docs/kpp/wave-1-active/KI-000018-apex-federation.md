# KI-000018 · Apex / Federation (शीर्ष संस्था / संघ)

**Definition:** An apex or federation is a higher-tier cooperative whose members are other cooperative societies — coordinating, supporting, and representing them at the district, state, or national level (for example a district central cooperative bank or a state marketing federation).

**Plain-language explanation:** It is a "society of societies" — primary societies join together to form a bigger body that supports and represents them.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** शीर्ष संस्था या संघ ऊँचे स्तर की सहकारी संस्था है जिसके सदस्य खुद दूसरी सहकारी समितियाँ होती हैं। यह उन्हें जिला, राज्य या राष्ट्रीय स्तर पर सहयोग, समर्थन और प्रतिनिधित्व देती है (जैसे जिला सहकारी बैंक या राज्य विपणन संघ)।

**English explanation:** A second/third-tier cooperative made up of member societies, providing them finance, marketing, or representation up the cooperative structure.

**Why it matters:** Federations consolidate member-society data and reporting; SahakarLekha supports multi-society consolidation and federation reports.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "A federation controls its member societies." → It supports/coordinates; member societies stay autonomous.
- ❌ "Individuals are members of a federation." → Its members are societies, not (usually) individuals.
