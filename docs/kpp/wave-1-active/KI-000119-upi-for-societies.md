# KI-000119 · UPI for Societies (समितियों के लिए UPI)

**Definition:** UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is an instant mobile-based bank-to-bank payment system; for societies it offers a fast, low-cost way to receive member payments and make payments, with each transaction reflected in the bank account.

**Plain-language explanation:** UPI lets members pay the society (and the society pay others) instantly from a phone — every UPI payment is a bank transaction to record.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** UPI (यूनिफाइड पेमेंट्स इंटरफ़ेस) मोबाइल से तुरंत बैंक-से-बैंक भुगतान की प्रणाली है। समितियों के लिए यह सदस्यों से भुगतान लेने और भुगतान करने का तेज़, सस्ता तरीका है। हर UPI लेन-देन बैंक खाते में दिखता है और उसे दर्ज करना ज़रूरी है।

**English explanation:** Instant phone-based bank transfers; a growing way for societies to collect dues digitally, recorded through the bank book.

**Why it matters:** Digital collection improves transparency and convenience. Recording UPI receipts/payments correctly keeps the bank book and reconciliation clean.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "UPI money doesn't need recording." → Every UPI receipt/payment is a bank transaction to enter.
- ❌ "UPI replaces the bank account." → UPI moves money in/out of the same bank account.
