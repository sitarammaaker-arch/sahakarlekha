# KI-000015 · Housing Cooperative Society (आवास सहकारी समिति)

**Definition:** A housing cooperative society is formed by members to acquire, build, or manage housing collectively — handling member maintenance contributions, common funds, and upkeep of shared property.

**Plain-language explanation:** It is the society that runs a housing colony or building: members pay maintenance, and the society looks after common areas, funds, and records.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** आवास सहकारी समिति सदस्यों द्वारा मिलकर मकान/भवन बनाने, खरीदने या उसका प्रबंधन करने के लिए बनती है। यह सदस्यों से रखरखाव (maintenance) लेती है, साझा फंड रखती है और साझा संपत्ति की देखभाल करती है।

**English explanation:** A members' cooperative that develops or manages housing, centred on maintenance dues, sinking/common funds, and shared-asset upkeep.

**Why it matters:** Housing societies use SahakarLekha for member dues, maintenance collection, funds, and reports — distinct from credit/trading societies.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "A housing society is a builder." → It is a members' body that manages/develops housing, not a commercial builder.
- ❌ "Maintenance money is the society's profit." → It is a fund for upkeep, accounted as members' contributions.
