# KI-000340 · Add a Bank Account (बैंक खाता कैसे जोड़ें)

**Definition:** Adding a bank account is the task of setting up a new bank in the books — entering the bank/branch name and its opening balance — so its transactions can be recorded and reconciled separately from cash and other banks.

**Plain-language explanation:** It is the task of adding a new bank in the software (name + opening balance) so you can record that bank's deposits and withdrawals.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** बैंक खाता जोड़ना यानी बही में नया बैंक सेट करना — बैंक/शाखा का नाम और उसका ओपनिंग बैलेंस डालना — ताकि उस बैंक की जमा-निकासी कैश व अन्य बैंकों से अलग दर्ज व मिलान (reconcile) हो सके। SahakarLekha में Bank Book से "+ नया बैंक" द्वारा यह जोड़ा जाता है।

**English explanation:** The step-by-step task of registering a new bank account (name + opening balance) so it is tracked and reconciled on its own.

**Why it matters:** Societies often hold several bank accounts. Adding each correctly keeps balances and reconciliation accurate.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "All banks share one entry." → Each bank account is added and tracked separately.
- ❌ "Opening balance can be skipped." → Enter the bank's opening balance so its records are complete.
