# KI-000065 · Voucher Approval Workflow (वाउचर स्वीकृति प्रक्रिया)

**Definition:** A voucher approval workflow is a control where a voucher entered by one person (the maker) must be reviewed and approved by another (the checker) before it is final — separating entry from authorisation to prevent errors and misuse.

**Plain-language explanation:** One person enters the voucher, a second person checks and approves it. This "maker-checker" step catches mistakes and stops unauthorised entries.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** वाउचर स्वीकृति प्रक्रिया एक नियंत्रण है जिसमें एक व्यक्ति (maker) द्वारा बनाया गया वाउचर दूसरे व्यक्ति (checker) की समीक्षा व स्वीकृति के बाद ही अंतिम होता है। एंट्री और मंज़ूरी अलग रखने से गलतियाँ व दुरुपयोग रुकते हैं।

**English explanation:** A maker-checker control in the software: entry and authorisation are split across users, strengthening internal control and the audit trail.

**Why it matters:** It builds trust and clean controls — auditors value separation of duties, and it reduces errors. SahakarLekha supports this workflow.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "Approval slows everything down." → It is a quick check that prevents costly errors and objections.
- ❌ "The same person can make and approve." → That defeats the control; maker and checker should differ.
