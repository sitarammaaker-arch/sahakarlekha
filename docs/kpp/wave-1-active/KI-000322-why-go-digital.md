# KI-000322 · Why Go Digital (डिजिटल क्यों अपनाएँ)

**Definition:** "Going digital" means moving a society's accounts from paper registers to accounting software, gaining speed, accuracy, instant reports, safer data, and easier audits.

**Plain-language explanation:** It is the case for switching from registers to software: less manual work, fewer mistakes, reports in one click, safer records, and smoother audits.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** "डिजिटल अपनाना" यानी समिति का हिसाब कागज़ी रजिस्टर से सॉफ़्टवेयर में लाना। इससे काम तेज़ होता है, गलतियाँ घटती हैं, रिपोर्ट एक क्लिक में मिलती है, डेटा ज़्यादा सुरक्षित रहता है और ऑडिट आसान हो जाता है। आसान शब्दों में — "रजिस्टर से सॉफ़्टवेयर पर आने के फ़ायदे"।

**English explanation:** The benefits-led rationale for adopting accounting software over manual registers — accuracy, instant reports, data safety, and audit-readiness.

**Why it matters:** Many societies still use paper. This articulates the value of switching — central to SahakarLekha's purpose and to a chairman's adoption decision.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "Digital is only for big or urban societies." → Even small/rural societies benefit, especially with Hindi-first, free tools.
- ❌ "Going digital means losing control." → It improves control through clear records, roles, and audit trails.
