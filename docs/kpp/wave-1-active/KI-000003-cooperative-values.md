# KI-000003 · Cooperative Values (सहकारिता के मूल्य)

**Definition:** Cooperative values are the shared beliefs a cooperative is built on — self-help, self-responsibility, democracy, equality, equity, and solidarity — along with ethical values like honesty, openness, and caring for others.

**Plain-language explanation:** They are the "character" of a cooperative — helping yourself by helping each other, treating members fairly, and being honest and open.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** सहकारिता के मूल्य वे साझा विश्वास हैं जिन पर हर सहकारी संस्था टिकी होती है — स्व-सहायता, स्व-जिम्मेदारी, लोकतंत्र, समानता, समता और एकजुटता; साथ ही ईमानदारी, खुलापन और दूसरों की परवाह जैसे नैतिक मूल्य।

**English explanation:** The underlying beliefs (self-help, democracy, equality, solidarity) and ethics (honesty, openness, caring) that the cooperative principles put into practice.

**Why it matters:** Values explain *why* cooperatives behave differently — democratic, fair, transparent — which in turn shapes their governance and accounting choices.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "Values and principles are the same." → Values are the beliefs; principles are how those beliefs are applied.
- ❌ "Values are just for the rulebook." → They guide real decisions like fair treatment and transparency.
