# KI-000101 · Cash Book (रोकड़ बही)

**Definition:** A cash book is the book that records all cash receipts and cash payments in date order and shows the cash balance in hand at any time.

**Plain-language explanation:** The cash book is the running record of all cash coming in and going out, so you always know how much cash the society should have.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** रोकड़ बही वह बही है जिसमें सारे नकद प्राप्ति (receipt) और नकद भुगतान (payment) तारीख के क्रम में लिखे जाते हैं, और किसी भी समय हाथ में रोकड़ का शेष दिखता है। आसान शब्दों में — "नकद का चलता-फिरता हिसाब"। इससे पता रहता है कि समिति के पास कितनी रोकड़ होनी चाहिए।

**English explanation:** A book of original entry for cash that doubles as a ledger account for cash — recording receipts and payments and revealing the cash balance.

**Why it matters:** The cash book is one of the most-used records and the basis of cash verification. A correct cash book is the foundation of clean cash handling.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "Cash book includes bank transactions." → Bank transactions belong to the bank book; SahakarLekha keeps them distinct.
- ❌ "Cash book is just a list." → It also shows the running cash balance, which should match physical cash.
