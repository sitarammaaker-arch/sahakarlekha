# KI-000013 · Marketing Cooperative Society (विपणन सहकारी समिति)

**Definition:** A marketing cooperative society helps members (usually farmers) sell their produce collectively — aggregating, sometimes processing, and marketing it (including under government procurement/MSP) to get better prices than selling alone.

**Plain-language explanation:** It is a society that sells members' produce together, so farmers get a better price and a reliable buyer instead of facing the market one by one.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** विपणन सहकारी समिति सदस्यों (आम तौर पर किसानों) की उपज को मिलकर बेचने में मदद करती है — उपज इकट्ठा कर, कभी-कभी प्रसंस्करण कर, और बाज़ार/सरकारी खरीद (MSP) में बेचकर। इससे अकेले बेचने की तुलना में किसानों को बेहतर दाम मिलता है।

**English explanation:** A cooperative that collectively markets members' produce (often via mandi/MSP procurement), giving farmers bargaining power and a stable market.

**Why it matters:** Marketing societies handle procurement, commission, and produce sales; SahakarLekha supports these (including MSP and commission-agent flows).

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "A marketing society buys produce to keep it." → It markets on behalf of members, often on commission.
- ❌ "It only deals in one crop." → Many handle several crops/commodities.
