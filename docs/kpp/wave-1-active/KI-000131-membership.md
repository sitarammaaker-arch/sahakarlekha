# KI-000131 · Membership (सदस्यता)

**Definition:** Membership is the status of belonging to a cooperative society — gained by formal admission per the byelaws — that gives a person ownership, voting rights, access to services, and a share in benefits and responsibilities.

**Plain-language explanation:** Membership is what makes someone part-owner of the society, with the right to vote, use services, and share in results — once they are formally admitted.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** सदस्यता का मतलब है किसी सहकारी समिति का सदस्य होना — जो समिति के नियमों के अनुसार विधिवत प्रवेश से मिलती है। इससे व्यक्ति को स्वामित्व, वोट का अधिकार, सेवाओं तक पहुँच और लाभ-ज़िम्मेदारी में हिस्सा मिलता है। आसान शब्दों में — "समिति का हिस्सा बनना"।

**English explanation:** The admitted status that confers ownership, voting, service access, and a share of benefits/obligations in a cooperative.

**Why it matters:** Membership is the entry point to a society and the basis of its member register, shares, and democratic control — usually the first data a society sets up.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "Using the society's services makes you a member." → Admission is a formal step under the byelaws.
- ❌ "Membership is only a benefit." → It also carries duties (e.g. participation, dues).
