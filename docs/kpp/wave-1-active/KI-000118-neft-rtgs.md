# KI-000118 · NEFT / RTGS (बैंक ट्रांसफर)

**Definition:** NEFT and RTGS are electronic bank-to-bank money transfer systems in India — NEFT settles in batches and suits routine transfers, while RTGS settles in real time and is used for larger-value transfers.

**Plain-language explanation:** They are two ways to send money directly from one bank account to another online — NEFT for everyday amounts, RTGS for big amounts that need to move immediately.

**Hindi explanation (आसान हिंदी):** NEFT और RTGS बैंक से बैंक पैसा भेजने के इलेक्ट्रॉनिक तरीके हैं। NEFT बैच में निपटता है और आम ट्रांसफर के लिए ठीक है; RTGS तुरंत (real-time) निपटता है और बड़ी रकम के लिए इस्तेमाल होता है। आसान शब्दों में — ऑनलाइन बैंक ट्रांसफर के दो तरीके।

**English explanation:** Electronic funds-transfer rails: NEFT (deferred/batch settlement) and RTGS (real-time, typically higher value), both reflected in the society's bank book.

**Why it matters:** Societies increasingly transact digitally; recording NEFT/RTGS correctly keeps the bank book accurate and reconciliation easy.

**Common misconceptions:**
- ❌ "NEFT and RTGS are the same." → They differ in settlement timing and typical use.
- ❌ "Digital transfers don't need a voucher." → Every transfer is still a transaction that must be recorded.
