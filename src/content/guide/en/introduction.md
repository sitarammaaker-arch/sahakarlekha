# Introduction

This book is for the managers, accountants, clerks, data-entry operators, auditors and new learners of **cooperative societies** (PACS, marketing societies, consumer stores, cooperative banks). It is at once a **training manual, a reference book, a user guide and an accounting textbook**.

Every chapter follows the same flow: *Learn → Do it in the SahakarLekha app → Example → ⚠️ Caution → 🔍 Audit note → ✅ Worked example → 📚 Case study → ❓ FAQ → ✏️ Exercises.* One imaginary society — **"Rania Cooperative"** — runs through the whole book.

> 💡 **Remember three principles:** (1) the app is a tool, the understanding is yours; (2) small correct habits save the books; (3) transparency is the very heart of cooperation.
