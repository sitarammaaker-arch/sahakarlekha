# Chapter 20 — Depreciation

> **After this chapter you will be able to:** SLM and WDV; per-asset WDV; accumulated depreciation; the journal; impact on the Balance Sheet.

## 20.1 What it is
The annual wear-and-tear expense on a fixed asset’s cost; the asset’s value falls and an expense is recorded.

## 20.2 Two methods

| Method | Basis |
|---|---|
| SLM (straight line) | A fixed % on original cost |
| WDV (written-down value) | A % on the book value (WDV) |

## 20.3 SLM example
₹1,00,000 godown, 10% SLM = ₹10,000 every year.

## 20.4 WDV example
₹1,00,000, 15% WDV: Year 1 ₹15,000; Year 2 15% × 85,000 = ₹12,750; Year 3 15% × 72,250 = ₹10,837…

## 20.5 Per-asset WDV (correct)
Each asset’s own accumulated depreciation is replayed (wdvAccumulatedBefore) — not category-wide.

> ⚠️ Audit fix #6: earlier WDV subtracted the whole category’s accumulated depreciation from one asset — now per-asset.

## 20.6 Journal
Dr Depreciation (5500) / Cr Accumulated Depreciation. On the Balance Sheet the asset is net (cost − accumulated).

## 20.7 In the app
Fixed Assets → asset, cost, method, rate → the depreciation journal is automatic at year-end.

## ⚠️ Common mistakes

| Mistake | Avoid by |
|---|---|
| Reducing the asset directly | The accumulated-depreciation account |
| Switching methods | Stick to one method |
| Category-wide WDV | Per-asset |

## 🔍 Audit note
"Depreciation at a fixed rate/method; per-asset WDV; accumulated depreciation deducted on the Balance Sheet."

## 📘 Standard
Method and rate consistent; per income-tax/cooperative rules.

## ✅ Worked example
₹50,000 equipment, 20% WDV: Year 1 ₹10,000 (balance 40,000); Year 2 ₹8,000 (balance 32,000). Dr Depreciation / Cr Accumulated Depreciation.

## 📚 Case study
WDV subtracted the whole category’s accumulated depreciation from one machine → a negative value. Per-asset replay fixed it.

## ❓ FAQ
**Q. SLM or WDV?** Per policy/rules; then stick with it.
**Q. A sold asset?** Stop depreciation; gain/loss separately.
