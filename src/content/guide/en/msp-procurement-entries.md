# Chapter 31 — Marketing/MSP Society Entries (HAFED, Gunny Bags, Driage)

> **After this chapter you will be able to:** record MSP procurement; pay farmers with recoveries; bill agency charges; account for gunny bags and driage; handle market fee and HRDF.

## 31.1 New accounts
| Code | Account | Category |
|---|---|---|
| 2402 | Farmer Payable (procurement) | Liability |
| 2405 | Market Fee Payable | Liability |
| 2406 | HRDF Payable | Liability |
| 3304 | HAFED/Agency Receivable | Asset |
| 3308 | Gunny Bags Stock | Asset |
| 5120 | Wheat Purchase (MSP) | Expense |
| 5310 | Labour/Freight | Expense |
| 5314 | Driage/Storage loss | Expense |
| 4310 | Procurement/Storage charges | Income |

## 31.2 MSP purchase (on actual weight)
100 qtl × ₹2,275: Dr Wheat Purchase (5120) 2,27,500 / Cr Farmer Payable (2402) 2,27,500.
> ⚠️ Record the purchase on the **actual weighment**, not the declared quantity.

## 31.3 Farmer payment — with recovery
Payable ₹2,27,500, old loan ₹40,000 recovered, balance by RTGS:
Dr Farmer Payable 2,27,500 / Cr Member Loan 40,000 / Cr Bank 1,87,500.

## 31.4 Procurement-related expenses
Labour/freight: Dr Labour (5310) / Cr Cash/Bank. Market fee: Dr Market Fee / Cr Market Committee Payable (2405). HRDF likewise (2406).

## 31.5 Gunny bags
Purchase: Dr Gunny Bags Stock (3308) / Cr Cash. Agency-supplied (free) bags go only into the **register**. On shortage: Dr Gunny-bag loss/officer-recovery / Cr HAFED (3304).

## 31.6 Agency charges & receipt
Charges (the society's earning): Dr HAFED (3304) / Cr Procurement Charges (4310). Receipt: Dr Bank / Cr HAFED.

## 31.7 Driage & stock shortage
Permissible driage: Dr Driage (5314) / Cr Stock. If excess → recover from the store-keeper (Recoverable Dr.).

## ⚠️ Common mistakes
| Mistake | Avoid by |
|---|---|
| Purchase on declared quantity | Use actual weighment |
| Treating charges as expense | They are income (receivable from agency) |
| Not counting gunny bags | Register: received = used + returned + balance |

## 🔍 Audit note
"J-Form/weighment slip = purchase; reconcile the HAFED account; reconcile gunny bags; permissible-driage limit."

## 📘 Cooperative standard
MSP purchase and sale are usually equal (price pass-through); the society's real income is the **charges**.

## ✅ Worked examples
Paddy 200 qtl × ₹2,300: Dr Paddy Purchase 4,60,000 / Cr Farmer Payable 4,60,000. Charges claim ₹85,000: Dr HAFED / Cr Procurement Charges 85,000.

## 📚 Case study
End-of-season gunny bags: received 10,000, used 9,500, returned 400, balance 80 → shortage 20 × ₹40 = ₹800 recovered from the store-keeper.

## ❓ FAQ
**Q. TDS on purchase from farmers?** Usually no; yes on arhtiya commission/contracts.
**Q. When are charges income?** Once entitlement is certain — as a receivable from HAFED.
