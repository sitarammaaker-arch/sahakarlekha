# Appendix D — Quick-Reference Card

## D.1 Debit-Credit

| Increase → Dr | Increase → Cr |
|---|---|
| Asset, expense | Liability, capital, income |

## D.2 Choosing a voucher

| Situation | Voucher |
|---|---|
| Money came in | Receipt |
| Money went out | Payment |
| Cash ↔ bank | Contra |
| Account adjustment only | Journal |

## D.3 Key formulas
- Stock = opening + purchases/+adj − sales/−adj (≥0)
- Stock value = qty × weighted-average cost
- Gross profit = sales − (opening + purchases − closing)
- Net surplus = (gross profit + other income) − expenses
- Assets = liabilities + capital
- Net GST = output − input
- Statutory reserve ≥ 25% of net surplus
- Closing cash = opening + receipts − payments
