# Chapter 33 — Rectification & Complex/Tricky Entries

> **After this chapter you will be able to:** fix common errors; make compound entries; handle tricky cases like "looks like an expense but is an asset".

## 33.1 The best way to correct
Edit/cancel the original voucher — stock, dues and supporting vouchers all cascade-correct (RULE 3). A manual reversing entry is the last resort.

## 33.2 Wrong amount
Under-recorded (₹4,500 gap): Dr Customer/Cash 4,500 / Cr Sales 4,500. Over-recorded: reverse.

## 33.3 Wrong account (correct side)
Rent posted to Repairs by mistake: Dr Rent / Cr Repairs.

## 33.4 Whole entry reversed
Dr-Cr swapped → use **double the amount** in the correct direction (one to cancel, one to record).

## 33.5 One side missing
The trial balance won't tally → add the missing side; use a temporary Suspense and zero it later.

## 33.6 Compound entry
One payment, several deductions — salary: Dr Salary / Cr TDS Payable + EPF Payable + Advance + Bank. Farmer payment likewise.

## 33.7 Tricky cases (decision key)
| Looks like | Actually | Because |
|---|---|---|
| Expense (security/guarantee deposit) | Asset | It comes back |
| Income (deposit/advance) | Liability | It must be returned |
| Purchase (furniture) | Asset | Durable, own-use |

## ⚠️ Common mistakes
| Mistake | Avoid by |
|---|---|
| Leaving it in Suspense | Find the cause and zero it |
| Correction without narration | "Correction: voucher no. __" |

## 🔍 Audit note
"An audit trail for every correction; Suspense zero at year-end; an error of principle is not caught by the trial balance."

## 📘 Cooperative standard
Corrections are board-informed where the amount is large; the edit history is preserved.

## ✅ Worked examples
Duplicate sale ₹4,000 cancelled: Dr Sales 4,000 / Cr Customer 4,000 (+ stock correction). Insurance claim for a pre-recorded loss: Dr Bank / Cr Insurance Claim Receivable.

## 📚 Case study
Cheque bounce: deposit (Dr Bank/Cr Contractor) → bounce (reverse) → charge (Dr Bank Charges) → cash recovery.

## ❓ FAQ
**Q. Trial balance tallies but an account is wrong?** Error of principle — check each balance for "reasonable?".
**Q. Prior year locked, an error found?** Prior-Period Adjustment this year; inform the auditor.
