# Chapter 8 — Purchase Management

> **After this chapter you will be able to:** cash/credit purchases; GST input credit; TDS deducted once; the Purchase Register.

## 8.1 Three effects
A purchase: increases stock, records cost, and money goes out / becomes payable to the supplier.

## 8.2 Cash / credit purchase
Cash: Dr Fertiliser Purchase (5110) 45,000 / Cr Cash 45,000 (stock +100).
Credit: Dr Fertiliser Purchase 45,000 / Cr IFFCO (2101) 45,000.

## 8.3 GST input credit (an asset)
₹10,000 + 18% GST, on credit:

| Account | Dr | Cr |
|---|---|---|
| Consumer Purchase (5112) | 10,000 | |
| GST Input Credit (3310) | 1,800 | |
| Supplier (2101) | | 11,800 |

> ⚠️ Don’t add GST into the purchase cost.

## 8.4 TDS — don’t deduct twice
grandTotal = taxable + GST − TDS. Payable = grandTotal; the deducted TDS sits separately in 2202.
₹1,00,000, TDS 0.1% = ₹100:

| Account | Dr | Cr |
|---|---|---|
| Purchase | 1,00,000 | |
| Supplier | | 99,900 |
| TDS Payable (2202) | | 100 |

## 8.5 In the app
Purchases → date, supplier, items → payment mode → Save (stock increases).

## 8.6 Per-item purchase account & stock
Activity accounts (5110…); a purchase updates stock and the cost rate.

## 8.7 Purchase Register
Date-wise purchases, GST, TDS, status. Export for GSTR-3B/TDS/audit.

## 8.8 Edit / delete
From Purchase Management — stock decreases, voucher cancelled, supplier ledger corrected.

## ⚠️ Common mistakes

| Mistake | Avoid by |
|---|---|
| GST into cost | 3310 (ITC) |
| TDS twice | Payable = grandTotal |
| Not choosing a supplier | Always |

## 🔍 Audit note
"Input GST is separate; TDS deduction and deposit correct; stock receipts reconcile with purchases."

## 📘 Cooperative standard
Purchases on the accrual basis; proper recording of ITC and TDS.

## ✅ Worked examples
Cash: Dr Seed Purchase 1,750 / Cr Cash 1,750. Credit+GST: Dr Pesticide 20,000 / Dr ITC 3,600 / Cr Supplier 23,600. TDS: Dr Purchase 2,00,000 / Cr Supplier 1,99,800 / Cr TDS Payable 200.

## 📚 Case study
TDS deducted twice left the voucher unbalanced. Setting Payable = grandTotal balanced it.

## ❓ FAQ
**Q. When does ITC come back?** Adjusted against output GST.
**Q. No GST?** Leave the field zero; enter directly.
