# Chapter 25 — Audit Preparation

> **After this chapter you will be able to:** audit types; the document file; common objections; the audit certificate; transparency.

## 25.1 Why audit
A cooperative society’s annual audit is statutory — members’ trust and departmental compliance.

## 25.2 The document file

| Document | Source |
|---|---|
| Trial balance, final accounts | App reports |
| Day Book/Ledger | App |
| Bank statement & reconciliation | Bank + app |
| Stock register | Inventory |
| Salary/TDS/GST | The relevant registers |
| Member register | Members |
| Appropriation resolution | AGM minutes |

## 25.3 Common objections & defence

| Objection | Defence |
|---|---|
| Trial balance won’t tally | Dr=Cr, opening |
| Stock mismatch | One formula, physical count |
| Taxes payable outstanding | Deposit on time |
| Reserve < 25% | Appropriation per rules |
| Cancelled vouchers in results | isDeleted filter |

## 25.4 Audit certificate
Reports → Audit Certificate → share capital (only subtype share_capital), funds, results — with a signature block.

> ⚠️ Audit fix #14: reserves must not be added to paid-up share capital — only the share_capital sub-type.

## 25.5 Audit trail
Every voucher’s editHistory; cancellations with a reason — instant answers to queries.

## ⚠️ Common mistakes

| Mistake | Avoid by |
|---|---|
| Last-minute prep | Keep up to date all year |
| Documents scattered | One file |
| Can’t explain a cancelled voucher | Show the trail |

## 🔍 Audit note
"Records well organised; objections minimal; audit trail complete; certificate standard-compliant."

## 📘 Cooperative standard
An annual audit and presentation of the report at the AGM are mandatory.

## ✅ Worked example
Rania: 7 documents in the file, trial balance balanced, stock physically reconciled, all payables deposited → a smooth audit.

## 📚 Case study
The auditor asked about a cancelled voucher; the trail gave the reason, date and person at once — the objection was settled.

## ❓ FAQ
**Q. How often is the audit?** Annually (per rules).
**Q. Where is the certificate?** The Audit Certificate report.
