# Chapter 30 — Comprehensive FAQ (the top 30 questions)

> The most-asked questions and answers in using SahakarLekha — a quick reference.

## Setup & basics
1. **How to create a new society?** Register → setup → financial year.
2. **Wrong financial year?** Change it in setup; reports are for that FY.
3. **Several societies?** Yes, each with separate data.
4. **Roles?** Admin/Accountant/Viewer + Maker-Checker.
5. **Forgot password?** "Forgot password" → email link.

## Entry
6. **Which voucher?** Money in = receipt; out = payment; cash↔bank = contra; else = journal.
7. **If Dr ≠ Cr?** The app won’t save.
8. **Is share capital income?** No — capital (1102).
9. **Admission fee?** Income.
10. **GST/TDS?** Liability — not income/cost.

## Stock
11. **35 here, 30 there?** Not any more — one formula.
12. **Closing 0 but bags?** If net qty is 0, stock 0 is correct.
13. **Sale above stock?** Blocked — purchase first.
14. **Stock value at which rate?** Weighted-average cost.
15. **Reducing an adjustment?** Add the "−" sign.

## Reports
16. **Trial balance won’t tally?** Check opening Dr = Cr.
17. **Balance Sheet won’t tally?** Surplus/stock/opening.
18. **Gross vs net profit?** Trading vs Income & Expenditure.
19. **R&P vs I&E?** Cash vs accrual.
20. **Closing stock in one place?** Yes — one value across the three reports.

## Tax
21. **CGST/SGST or IGST?** Intra-state vs inter-state.
22. **ITC?** Adjusted against output (3310).
23. **TDS twice?** No — payable = grandTotal.
24. **TDS quarter?** From the payment date.
25. **GST on a discount?** On the net value.

## Year-end & security
26. **Order of appropriation?** Reserve (≥25%) → dividend → patronage → remainder.
27. **Is dividend an expense?** No — an appropriation.
28. **After FY-Lock?** Mutations are blocked.
29. **Is data safe?** Cloud + RLS; regular PDF backups.
30. **Saved locally but not in cloud?** Two-step save + rollback; on a failure, see the toast and do it again.
