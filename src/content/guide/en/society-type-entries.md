# Chapter 32 — Society-Type Entries (Dairy, Consumer, Weaver, Fishery, Labour, SHG)

> **After this chapter you will be able to:** record the distinctive entries of every kind of society — dairy, consumer, weaver, fishery, labour-contract, self-help group, housing.

## 32.1 Dairy society
Purchase: Dr Milk Purchase / Cr Milk Producers Payable (2403). Union sale: Dr Union (3303) / Cr Milk Sales (4106). Cattle-feed on credit: Dr Member / Cr Feed Sales. Payment made net of the feed recovery.
> Margin = sale rate − purchase rate (per litre).

## 32.2 Consumer store
Category-wise cash sale: Dr Cash / Cr Grocery Sales + FMCG Sales + Grain Sales. PDS commission: Dr Receivable / Cr PDS Commission Income. Expiry: Dr Stock Loss / Cr Stock.

## 32.3 Weaver / handloom
Finished-goods purchase: Dr Cloth Purchase / Cr Weaver Payable. Yarn issued: Dr Yarn Issued / Cr Yarn Stock. Weaving wages: Dr Weaving / Cr Bank.

## 32.4 Fishery
Pond lease: Dr Lease / Cr Bank. Seed/feed: Dr Seed-Feed / Cr Cash. Auction sale: Dr Cash / Cr Fish Sales.

## 32.5 Labour contract (LCS)
RA bill: Dr Department (3303) / Cr Contract Receipts. Wages: Dr Labour Wages / Cr Bank. SD deduction: Dr Department-SD (asset) / Cr Department.

## 32.6 SHG / self-help group
Monthly savings: Dr Cash / Cr Members Savings (liability). Bank-linkage loan: Dr Bank / Cr SHG Bank Loan. Inter-loan: Dr Member Loan / Cr Cash.

## 32.7 Housing society
Member instalment: Dr Bank / Cr Members Contribution. Land development: Dr Land Development / Cr Bank. Maintenance: Dr Cash / Cr Maintenance Charges.

## ⚠️ Common mistakes
| Mistake | Avoid by |
|---|---|
| Union sale as a cash sale | Union receivable (credit) |
| Producer payable as income | It is a liability (to be paid) |
| Forgetting the feed recovery | Adjust it in the payment |

## 🔍 Audit note
"Milk fat/SNF register = the basis of payment; PDS lifting vs distribution; LCS measurement book."

## 📘 Cooperative standard
Every society type is member-service centred; the specific register (milk, yarn, ration) is mandatory.

## ✅ Worked examples
Milk 800 litres × ₹40: Dr Milk Purchase 32,000 / Cr Producers Payable 32,000. SHG savings ₹2,000: Dr Cash / Cr Members Savings 2,000.

## 📚 Case study
Dairy: 5,000 L bought at ₹40, sold at ₹44 → margin ₹20,000; ₹15,000 feed-credit recovered from the payment.

## ❓ FAQ
**Q. Where does cattle-feed on credit go?** Dr Member (income on sale); recover from the next milk payment.
**Q. The amount retained in LCS?** Security deposit (asset), refunded later.
