# Chapter 22 — Profit Distribution (Appropriation)

> **After this chapter you will be able to:** the statutory reserve of 25%; dividend and bonus; patronage rebate; distributable surplus; the over-appropriation guard.

## 22.1 What is appropriation
Sharing the net surplus per the rules — first the reserve, then dividend/patronage/funds.

## 22.2 The order

| Order | Item | Basis |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Statutory reserve | ≥ 25% of net surplus |
| 2 | Education/other fund | Per rules |
| 3 | Dividend | On share capital |
| 4 | Patronage rebate | On members’ transactions |
| 5 | Remainder | Accumulated surplus |

## 22.3 Statutory reserve
₹40,000 surplus → ≥ ₹10,000 (25%) to reserve: Dr Surplus-Appropriation / Cr Statutory Reserve (1201).

## 22.4 Dividend (payable)
Dr Surplus-Appropriation / Cr Dividend Payable (2103); on payment Dr 2103 / Cr Bank.

## 22.5 Patronage rebate (appropriation, not expense)
A return in proportion to a member’s transactions: Dr Surplus-Appropriation / Cr Patronage Rebate Payable.

> ⚠️ Do not treat dividend/patronage/bonus as **expense** — they are appropriations (audit fix C-3/C-4, #12).

## 22.6 Distributable surplus & over-appropriation guard
Distributable = surplus − already-posted dividend/bonus. The app blocks when the remainder would go negative.

> ⚠️ Audit fix #13: distributable earlier didn’t subtract the posted amounts and didn’t block over-appropriation — now both.

## 22.7 In the app
Profit Distribution → surplus → enter reserve %/dividend/patronage → Post only when "remaining ≥ 0".

## ⚠️ Common mistakes

| Mistake | Avoid by |
|---|---|
| Skipping the reserve | ≥25% mandatory |
| Dividend as expense | Appropriation |
| Distributing more than the surplus | The guard applies |

## 🔍 Audit note
"Statutory reserve ≥25% transferred; dividend/patronage in appropriation; distribution within the surplus."

## 📘 Cooperative standard
≥25% of net profit to the statutory reserve; dividend AGM-approved.

## ✅ Worked example
Surplus ₹40,000: reserve 25% ₹10,000; dividend ₹6,000; patronage ₹4,000; remainder ₹20,000 to accumulated surplus. Each: Dr Appropriation / Cr the relevant account.

## 📚 Case study
Bonus was added to expense 5207 → surplus understated, payable wrong. Moving it to payable and the 25% reserve fixed it.

## ❓ FAQ
**Q. If there’s a deficit?** No appropriation; adjust against accumulated surplus.
**Q. Dividend in cash at once?** First payable (2103), then payment.
