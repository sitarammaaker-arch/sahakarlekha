# Chapter 35 — Expense Dictionary & Misc Cooperative Activities

> **After this chapter you will be able to:** make the correct entry for every kind of expense; and record service income such as rent, hire and storage.

## 35.1 The universal expense formula
Every expense: **Dr expense account / Cr Cash/Bank/Payable**. If on credit, use the payable; exceptions below.

## 35.2 Common expenses (dictionary)
Water, sanitation, advertisement, newspaper, postage/courier, conveyance, security guard, godown rent, weighing, commission paid, festival, generator fuel, AMC, software subscription, training, federation membership, licence, professional tax, locker rent, refreshment — all `Dr [expense] / Cr Cash/Bank`.

## 35.3 "Looks like an expense but is an asset"
Electricity/rent security deposits, advances (to supplier/staff), advance tax → **assets** (refundable/adjustable), not expenses.

## 35.4 Service income (misc activity)
Tractor/machine hire: Dr Cash / Cr Hire Income. Godown storage: Dr Bank / Cr Storage Income. Hall rent: Dr Cash / Cr Rent Income. Weighbridge: Dr Cash / Cr Weighbridge Income.
> Goods belonging to the farmer/customer are **not** in society stock — only in a register.

## 35.5 Fund-based
Welfare-fund contribution: Dr Cash / Cr Welfare Fund (liability). Aid from the fund: Dr Welfare Fund / Cr Bank (not an expense — use of the fund).

## 35.6 Investment & dividend
Apex-bank shares: Dr Investment / Cr Bank. Dividend received: Dr Bank / Cr Dividend Received (income).

## ⚠️ Common mistakes
| Mistake | Avoid by |
|---|---|
| Security deposit as expense | It is an asset |
| Fund aid as expense | Use of fund (Dr the fund) |
| Service income as a sale | A separate income account |

## 🔍 Audit note
"Capital vs revenue separation; fund use as per rules; hire/storage registers."

## 📘 Cooperative standard
Each fund has its own purpose and rules of use; inter-fund transfer needs board approval.

## ✅ Worked examples
Tractor hire ₹2,000: Dr Cash / Cr Hire Income 2,000. Electricity security deposit ₹5,000: Dr Electricity Security Deposit (asset) / Cr Bank.

## 📚 Case study
Welfare fund: contribution ₹5,000 (liability up) → ₹3,000 aid to a sick member (Dr fund, Cr bank) — no impact on the P&L.

## ❓ FAQ
**Q. Where does software subscription go?** Software Subscription (expense); if ITC-eligible, keep GST separate.
**Q. Security deposit refunded?** Dr Bank / Cr Security Deposit (the asset reduces).
