# Chapter 27 — Golden Rules of Cooperative Accounting

> **After this chapter you will be able to:** principles and cautions worth remembering day to day.

## 27.1 Ten golden rules

| # | Rule |
|---|---|
| 1 | Every transaction Dr = Cr |
| 2 | Share capital = capital, not income |
| 3 | Admission fee = income |
| 4 | GST/TDS = liability, not income/cost |
| 5 | Closing stock = at cost, one formula |
| 6 | Dividend/patronage/bonus = appropriation |
| 7 | Reserve ≥ 25% of net surplus |
| 8 | Don’t mix capital with revenue |
| 9 | Cancel, don’t delete |
| 10 | If a save fails, do it again |

## 27.2 Daily habits
Enter every day; month-end trial balance and bank reconciliation; pay taxes on time; back up regularly.

## 27.3 Big cautions
No overselling; a contra is not a receipt; no double TDS; stock not in two places.

## ✅ The "10-second check" (before saving)
Right voucher type? Right accounts? Dr = Cr? Right date/FY? Party/member linked? Clear narration?

## 📚 Case study
A printed copy of these rules on the wall — a new clerk’s mistakes fell dramatically.
