# Chapter 11 — Salary Management

> **After this chapter you will be able to:** employee master and slips; gross/deductions/net; the salary entry; depositing EPF/ESI/PT/TDS.

## 11.1 Why salary is special
Gross − deductions = net. The deductions are the society’s liability (payable to the government).

## 11.2 Employee master
Salary → Add Employee: name, role, gross salary, EPF/ESI/PT applicable?

## 11.3 Components

| Component | Example (₹) |
|---|---|
| Gross | 20,000 |
| − EPF | 1,800 |
| − ESI | 150 |
| − PT | 200 |
| − TDS | 500 |
| = Net | 17,350 |

## 11.4 Making a slip
Employee + month → deductions → net → make slip → PDF.

## 11.5 The entry

| Account | Dr | Cr |
|---|---|---|
| Salary (expense) | 20,000 | |
| EPF Payable (2203) | | 1,800 |
| ESI Payable (2204) | | 150 |
| PT Payable (2207) | | 200 |
| TDS Payable (2202) | | 500 |
| Salary Payable/Cash | | 17,350 |

> 💡 The full gross is the expense; the employee gets the net.

## 11.6 Depositing deductions
Dr EPF/ESI/PT/TDS Payable / Cr Bank.

## 11.7 Salary register
Month-wise slips, deductions, net; "EPF Payable" = balance of account 2203.

## 11.8 Bonus/gratuity
Regular bonus = expense (5207); bonus out of profit = **appropriation/payable**; gratuity = provision (5206).

> ⚠️ Don’t put profit-bonus in expense 5207 — into payable.

## ⚠️ Common mistakes

| Mistake | Avoid by |
|---|---|
| Treating net as the expense | The full gross is the expense |
| Not depositing deductions | Check 2202–2207 |
| Profit-bonus in expense | Into payable |

## 🔍 Audit note
"Statutory deductions deducted and deposited on time; payable accounts verified."

## 📘 Labour standard
EPF/ESI/TDS deduction and deposit by the due date are statutory obligations.

## ✅ Worked example
Gross ₹15,000; EPF ₹1,200, PT ₹150 → net ₹13,650; Dr Salary 15,000 / Cr EPF Payable 1,200 / Cr PT Payable 150 / Cr Cash 13,650.

## 📚 Case study
Treated only the net as the expense → deductions-payable not shown, not deposited on time. After the fix, correct.

## ❓ FAQ
**Q. EPF/ESI not applicable?** Keep zero; gross = net.
**Q. Employer’s share?** A separate expense entry.
